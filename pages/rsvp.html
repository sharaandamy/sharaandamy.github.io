FAQ

Will you have a registry?

We haven't decided yet. What we want more than anything else is for you to attend and have a good time. If we decide to have one, a registry link will be provided once formal invitations have been sent.

Are kids welcome?

Of course! Our young nieces and nephew will most likely be attending, so feel free to bring your kids. Be sure to supervise them, since mountain terrain can have many steep falls.

Is there a dress code?

Cocktail attire (nice, but not super formal). The Seattle area generally has sunny, mild weather in the Summer but there can be unpredictable heat waves. As the wedding will be in the mountains, it should be on the cooler end.

Additionally, make sure to bring a pair of shoes suitable for walking on the dirt or small rocks that will likely be at the top of the gondola. Sun protection, whether a hat and sunglasses or sunscreen (or both!) is probably also a good idea.

How is parking?

Super plentiful. The ski resort accommodates thousands of visitors every weekend during the Winter, and is much less crowded during the summer.
