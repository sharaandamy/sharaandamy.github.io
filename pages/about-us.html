About Us

Our story began on OkCupid of all places, because where else are 2 shy LGBT girls going to meet? After a couple weeks of sending very long messages to one another, we were going to meet up in person when disaster struck! Shara's sister Kayla (in Singapore at the time) came down with dengue fever, so Shara had to travel and take care of her.

Amy was obviously a little skeptical of this fantastical story, but we kept writing to one another anyway. Fast forward another couple weeks to when Shara was back in Seattle. Amy then got sick right before the first date, which obviously led to making a bad first impression. Those extra weeks of messages paid off, because they led to getting a chance for a much better second impression.

We've stayed together through 4 years of long-distance while Shara attended medical school and are together in Seattle again for a couple years now. Our travels have taken us on several beautiful backpacking trips in the PNW, as well as to Europe and Patagonia... with many more adventures to come!
