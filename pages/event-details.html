Ceremony

Where: Mt. Rainier View Platform
When: Afternoon, Specifics TBD

The ceremony will take place on the platform at the top of the scenic gondola. There will be breathtaking views of all of the surrounding mountains. Please arrive with enough time to account for riding the gondola up to the platform. Gondola tickets will be provided.

Reception

Where: Crystal Mountain Lodge, Bullwheel
When: Evening, Specifics TBD

The reception will take place in the Crystal Mountain ski lodge on the Bullwheel level.

Dress

Cocktail attire (nice, but not super formal)

Additionally, make sure to bring a pair of shoes suitable for walking on the dirt or small rocks that will likely be at the top of the gondola. Sun protection, whether a hat and sunglasses or sunscreen (or both!) is probably also a good idea.
